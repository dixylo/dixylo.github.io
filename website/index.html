About me

My name is Xiaofeng LIN. It reads in Mandarin like shjouFUHNG leen or /ɕĭɑʊ˨˩fəŋ˥ lin˧˥/ if you know IPA (For Xiao, insert a very short ee between sh and ou and drop t when you say shout). Xiaofeng is my given name and Lin is my family name, but I usually accept people call me Lin for their convenience (Xiao looks indeed weird and perplexing for English speakers). And I sometimes take Lynn as my English name, which sounds similar to Lin. I come from Manchuria, which is now the northeastern part of China. I am now a student of Unitec, pursuing a Graduate Diploma in Computing.
